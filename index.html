Bio

My name is Sauda Afrose, and I’m currently pursuing a degree in education with a focus on inclusion and equity. I’ve always been interested in understanding how culture shapes learning experiences, especially for immigrant and multilingual students.

Due to that interest, I’ve been exploring ways educators can build more culturally responsive classrooms. I also enjoy combining creativity and advocacy—through projects, art, and writing—to highlight voices that often go unheard.
